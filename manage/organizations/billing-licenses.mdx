The Billing and Licenses area lets organization admins review subscription status, billing details, and license information tied to their cloud organization.

## What You Can Expect

- View current plan and subscription status
- Review billing and renewal details
- Check license-related organization information

## Need Help?

If you need billing or licensing support for Pangolin Cloud, contact the Pangolin team from the dashboard support options or via the support channels listed in this documentation.
